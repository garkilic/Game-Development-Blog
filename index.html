What is this?

Most resources for learning something new (like coding) are made by people doing a retrospective on how they learned. They give you advice and resources about what helped them, and in many ways, that is incredibly helpful.

I always thought it would be interesting to document my journey learning something completely new, but there had never been any topic I was interesting in pursuing from the beginning. I wanted to explain my process, document my successes and failures, have a repository of work done, as well as have it all time boxed.

Recently, I found myself incredibly interested in the world of game development. More specifically, I started watching videos about VR Chat and the different ways that they built features and technology that people really loved. I have always wanted to build technology that really spoke to people, and I thought that building in VR, more specifically in the vein of VR Chat, could be something I would really enjoy. So, with that idea, I started gathering resources to start learning.
