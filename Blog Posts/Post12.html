I missed you.

Date: Nov. 28th, 2022

I feel like I always hit this point with blogging. It's fun when it's easy and the words flow effortlessly but a little bit more difficult when I get busy or distracted.

That being said I did miss writing here (I only missed one week, but still).

A lot has happened since I wrote last.

I got a new job which I am incredibly excited about.

Anna got me a VR headset which is even crazier than I expected it to be.

Just a ton of good stuff has been happening which is just the best.

I want to write about more of this in detail, and I will, but not this week.

I feel like it's been hard to write in depth blog posts every week. Most of my posts aren't even about development, which I think is fine, but still, I want what I write to be more in depth.

So, moving forward, I am going to have more focused blog posts that I will write every two weeks. I imagine I will maintain this informal tone, but I want these "blog coversations" to be more technical. I want to writing here to help make the information more concrete, not just be a place for my rambling, so that's what I will be going for.

My next post will be Dec. 12th, so look out for that post!

Thanks for reading and staying up to date.

Thanks for reminding me to write, Blaine.

Cheers.

-Griffin
