My Brain Doesn't Work

Date: Nov. 14th, 2022

I feel like my brain has been living in some primordial fluid over the past week. Just kind of floating around, not capable of much. Not to make it sound like I have been doing nothing, I have been doing a ton, I have just been incredibly tired.

I honestly don't know how people are able to juggle all of these responsibilities (work, hobbies, exercise, etc.) and that's just as a 25 year old. I don't even have that many things to worry about and I am already tired.

This past week I focused on a lot of things that were not development. I took a bit of a break to relax my brain. I focused on other endeavours, ran a lot, explored new opportunities, watched videos about VR, it was a nice week.

I focused a lot of what's going on in the Crypto space actually. I never really "Got" what crypto or Web3 was about (not for a lack of trying), and now that it's all coming down in terms of frozen withdrawals and FTX going from "liquid" to bankrupt in a matter of days, I cannot say I am surprised. It really bums me out that for these silly technologies to crash that real people have to suffer the consequences.

I think this post will act more as a time capsule then something to learn from and follow.

What Did I Accomplish This Week?

  • Watched a lot of interesting videos on VR this week

What Am I Stuck On?

  • I feel like I can't concentrate when I am tired.
