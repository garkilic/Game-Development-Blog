Trying

Date: Oct. 24th, 2022

I don't know what I thought this blog was going to be when I started it. I think that I was hoping it would be more technical. So far that does not to appear the case.

I am still working on stuff though, the VR dream is not dead. Almost every day I am working on one project or another. This past week I finished the Unity Fundamentals course which taught me about sound design, assets, and physics.

I am closer to my goals than I was last week, but some days it doesn't feel like it. I try to be positive but some days the reality of becoming a VR developer (or working in the space) seems farther and farther away. Some days I don't even know if it's what I want to do.

Don't get me wrong, I love what I am building, I love my projects, working in Unity, even the VR space itself, I just get lost in what the end result is, and maybe that's the problem. Maybe I should stop focusing on that. I know I should stop worrying in general (if only it were that easy) but I get bogged down in the details.

As I write now, my head gets clearer. That's probably my favorite part about this blog, that it's a judgement free place to put my thoughts. Maybe it doesn't matter this blog and journey isn't what I thought it was going to be. It's not to late to change it, or keep refining it.

That should be the lesson to take away from this, that it's not too late. I am working on something I genuinely love and that is a huge accomplishment.

What Did I Accomplish This Week?

  • Completed the Unity Essentials Course
  • Developed two new game ideas

What Am I Stuck On?

    • Still stuck on the computer science fundamentals. I cannot find a compelling way to learn this stuff.
    • I am having difficulty organizing my time.

    Talk next week,

    -Griffin
