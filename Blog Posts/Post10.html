Beginning The Work

Date: Nov. 7th, 2022

I feel like I am getting this. I finally feel like I can go out on my own and build what's in my head (albeit with a ton of Googling), and that's an awesome feeling.

It's weird, sometimes I feel like I am at the beginning of something and sometimes I feel like I am banging my head against the wall. I love to do this stuff, but it feels like it's not leading anywhere (or I will never get there).

It's such a bummer I feel this way because the work that I am putting into this is actually really fun. Maybe that's a part of the problem, so far in my life I have trudged through things that aren't fun in my job, in my life, because I thought I had too, and now it feels like it's too late. Or maybe you have to do that stuff you don't like to figure out what you want to do.

All of this to say, I wish I could work on this stuff all day. I know some people make games for a living, but it feels like the dream like becoming a famous actor is a dream or sailing the world is a dream. But even as I write that I realize how silly it is because those dreams are 100x bigger then "spend all day building games". There are tons of developers, I know a lot of them, some are even self-taught. If they can do it, why can't I?

\

When I talk about this with other people they talk about the bigger picture, which makes sense (from an outside perspective). I think it's the day-to-day, the incrememntal learning, the lack of concrete path, that makes it difficult.
