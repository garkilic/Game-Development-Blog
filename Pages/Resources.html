How to make a Video Game

This is a playlist of videos from Brackeys, a well known and highly respected teacher in the Unity tutorial space. This is a playlist of videos covering covering the basics of making a simple game and at the end, you will have your own game you can download yourself. The Unity version being used here is a little out of date, but it's the best tutorial playlist I have found so far.

Amount of content: +50 hours

Cost: Free

Making a Multiplayer FPS in Unity
